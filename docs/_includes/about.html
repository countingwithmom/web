About

Lynn is a children's book author with a passion for making learning math fun and accessible for young readers. Growing up, she was fascinated by the world of math and loved exploring its many concepts and applications.
Now, as a parent, Lynn is excited to share her love of math with her daughter and other young readers. She believes that learning math should be natural and enjoyable, and that children can develop a strong foundation in math through engaging stories and activities. Through her work, Lynn hopes to inspire a new generation of young learners to embrace discovering the world of math.

Elettra is an illustrator and also a mother. She is a very talented freelance illustrator from Vicenza, Italy. She obtained her degree in Visual Arts and a Master's degree in Economics and Management of Arts. She enjoys bringing stories to life with vivid colors and textures that captivate young readers.
Prior working on the Counting With Mom project, Elettra worked with a diverse range of clients across various industries to fulfill their digital illustration needs. She is enthusiastic about bringing her unique perspective and creativity to our project.
